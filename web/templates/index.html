Japan FIRE — Rules of Thumb

3–3.5% Safe withdrawal rate for Japan
Lower than the US 4% rule due to Japan's lower expected equity returns and higher bond allocation norms
Year 1 Residence tax shock
You'll pay full residence tax on your last salary in year 1 of retirement. Budget for it.
60歳 iDeCo unlock age
iDeCo is completely illiquid before 60. Pre-60 FIRE means NISA + taxable must cover the gap.
1,800万円 新NISA lifetime cap
1.2M/yr tsumitate + 2.4M/yr growth frame. Tax-free growth and withdrawal, fully liquid.
